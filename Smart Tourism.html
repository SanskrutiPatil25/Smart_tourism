Top Destinations

Agra

Home of the iconic Taj Mahal and Mughal heritage.

Kerala

Known for serene backwaters, Ayurveda, and lush greenery.

Rajasthan

Land of majestic forts, deserts, and vibrant culture.

Goa

Famous for its beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.

Varanasi

Spiritual city on the banks of the Ganges with ancient temples.

Mysore

Known for grand palaces and rich cultural heritage in Karnataka.

Ajintha caves

The Ajanta Caves are 30 rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments dating from the second century BCE to about 480 CE in Aurangabad district of Maharashtra state in India. Ajanta Caves are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Darjeeling

Famous hill station known for tea plantations and stunning views.

Hampi

UNESCO heritage site with ancient ruins and temples.
